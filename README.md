### 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Data Engineer** who enjoys turning messy data into systems that actually make sense. I build dashboards and solutions that help businesses make smarter decisions.

I also love **mobile development** — I enjoy building apps that people can actually use, not just talk about.

I care about clean structure, efficient processes, and results you can measure. If something takes 3 hours to run, I'm already thinking about how to make it take 10 minutes.

Still learning. Always improving. Slightly obsessed with fixing chaos in data and building cool things on the side.
